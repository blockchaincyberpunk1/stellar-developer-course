Setting Up Stellar Core

Course: Stellar Developer Course

Assignment Objective: The objective of this assignment is to enable students to set up a Stellar Core node, gaining hands-on experience with Stellar's core infrastructure.

Instructions for Students:

Assignment Task:

Setting Up Stellar Core

In this assignment, you will have the opportunity to set up your own Stellar Core node. This hands-on experience will give you valuable insights into the inner workings of the Stellar network.

Assignment Requirements:

Environment Selection (20 points):

Choose whether you want to set up your Stellar Core node on your local development environment or connect to the Stellar testnet.
Justify your choice by briefly explaining the benefits and challenges of your selected environment.
Node Setup (40 points):

Follow a step-by-step guide or tutorial to set up a Stellar Core node on your chosen environment.
Document the installation process, including any configuration settings you choose.
Connection to Testnet (30 points):

If you opt for setting up a testnet node, ensure that you successfully connect your node to the Stellar testnet.
Document the steps you took to establish this connection.
Challenges and Solutions (10 points):

Describe any challenges you encountered during the setup process and provide solutions or workarounds you applied.
Reflection (10 points):

Reflect on the experience of setting up your Stellar Core node.
Discuss what you've learned and how this knowledge can benefit your future projects or development on Stellar.
Submission Guidelines:

Submit a document (e.g., PDF) that includes your environment choice, documentation of the node setup process, details about the testnet connection (if applicable), challenges faced, and your reflection.
Include any configuration files or scripts used during the setup.
Grading Rubric:

Environment Selection (20 points)
Node Setup (40 points)
Connection to Testnet (30 points)
Challenges and Solutions (10 points)
Reflection (10 points)
